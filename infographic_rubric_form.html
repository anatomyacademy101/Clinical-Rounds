Assignment Instructions (Step 8)

When you have completed your rounds of all patients, each group member will research one of the five diseases and create an infographic for that disease. See the attached example for Sarcopenia. You must include all of the following for full credit:

  • An informational overview of the disease.
  • A detailed description of the signs and symptoms of the disease.
  • The etiology of the disease — as if you were explaining it to the patient and their family.
  • How the disease is diagnosed (testing).
  • The prognosis of the disease.
