How the activity works

Your group of 5 has two roles: 2 Medical Team members stay on this device the whole time. 3 students rotate as patients, each playing a different character across 5 rounds. After each round, use the ⇄ Rotate Roles tab to hand the patient device to the next student — chart data is never lost.

Procedure
1 Setup — Overview tab
Enter all 5 group member names in the Group Members section above and click Save Names.
Go to ⇄ Rotate Roles and select which student will be the first patient.
Hand a second device to that student. They open this app, select I am a Patient, and choose their room.
2 Interview the Patient — Patient Rooms tab
Select the active room tile to open the interview guide.
Ask each question out loud to the patient student. Check off the question as you ask it.
Type the patient's answer into the field beneath each question.
3 Complete the Medical Chart — Medical Charts tab
Select the patient from the dropdown. Vital signs are pre-loaded; interview answers auto-fill the history fields.
Review and edit the auto-filled fields. Add any details the patient mentioned that weren't captured.
Click ✦ Generate Narrative to produce an AI clinical intake note from the interview answers.
Click Save Chart when done.
4 Narrow Your Diagnosis — Disease Reference tab
Open the Disease Reference tab. Review the disease cards and click each card's status button to mark diseases as Eliminated, Possible, or Likely.
Open disease cards to compare diagnostic tests and — if a chart is open — view this patient's actual test results.
Narrow your list to no more than 3 possibilities before choosing a final diagnosis.
5 Record Your Diagnosis — Medical Charts tab
Return to the chart and fill in Final Diagnosis, Evidence, Treatment Plan, and Prognosis.
Save the chart, then go to Team Tracker and confirm the diagnosis there.
6 Rotate to the Next Patient — ⇄ Rotate Roles tab
The patient student clicks ✓ Round Done on their device.
On the team device, go to ⇄ Rotate Roles, select the next patient student, and click Start Round.
Repeat steps 2–5 for all 5 rooms. Chart data from completed rounds is never affected.
7 Infographic Assignment — after all 5 rounds
Each group member claims one of the 5 diagnosed diseases.
Create a patient-facing infographic for your disease. Requirements are listed in the Team Tracker tab.

Infographic Assignment

Once all five patients are diagnosed, each of the 5 group members claims one disease from the diagnoses made during rounds and creates a patient-facing infographic. Each infographic must include:

  • Informational overview of the disease
  • Detailed description of signs and symptoms
  • Etiology — causes, risk factors, and mechanisms
  • How the disease is diagnosed (testing methods)
  • Prognosis and long-term outlook for the patient

Each group member should choose a different disease. See your teacher for the submission format.

Rotate Roles

Track who plays each patient and hand off the patient role between rounds. Chart data is never affected by rotation.

ℹ️
When a round is finished, the patient student presses ✓ Round Done on their screen — or you can trigger the hand-off from here. Either way, only the active patient cue card changes. All interview notes and chart data stay intact.
